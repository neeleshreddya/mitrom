Qualcomm Off Campus Drive 2025: Associate Engineer (SW/HW) – Freshers Apply Now!

Join Telegram Join WhatsApp Instagram

Are you a graduate looking to launch your technology career at a global giant? Qualcomm’s Off Campus Drive 2025 offers the perfect launchpad for both software and hardware enthusiasts. With a worldwide reputation for driving wireless, mobile, and AI innovation, Qualcomm unlocks opportunities for fresh talent via its promising Associate Engineer positions. This detailed guide gives you all you need to know about eligibility, job roles, salary, and how to apply for Qualcomm’s latest Off Campus Drive.

About the Qualcomm Off Campus Drive 2025

Qualcomm is a leader in mobile technology—accelerating innovation across devices and platforms. For over 30 years, Qualcomm has pioneered advances in wireless, networking, multimedia, and compute. In 2025, the company is seeking new graduates with curiosity, strong technical foundation, and a passion for technology to become part of their engineering ecosystem, working on cutting-edge chips, software stacks, and real-world solutions that affect billions of users and industries worldwide.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Engineering, Information Systems, CS, ECE or a related area
  • Recent/pass-out batches including 2025 graduates welcome
  • Freshers and candidates with less than 1 year experience eligible
  • Solid understanding of basic programming and engineering concepts (C/C++/OOP for SW; Verilog, RTL for HW)
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities

Compensation and Locations

  • Expected CTC: ₹15–20 LPA (as per sources and previous batches)
  • Work Locations: Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, and Noida (candidates may be assigned based on preference and business need)

About the Role: What You’ll Work On

Qualcomm offers two tracks—Associate Engineer Software (SW) and Hardware (HW). Here’s a breakdown:

Software Role (SW):
  • Develop, test, and optimize device drivers and embedded firmware
  • Participate in mobile development—Android, Windows, Linux
  • Support multimedia systems (audio, video, imaging), UI, and OS development
  • Work on wireless protocols (LTE/5G/WiFi/Bluetooth), kernel drivers, and OS
  • Innovate in IoT, VR/AR, autonomous systems, and next-gen applications
Hardware Role (HW):
  • SoC/ASIC design, validation, and simulation for wireless and mobile
  • RF/Analog/Digital IC, board, FPGA, and CAD design
  • Debugging, system integration, physical and logical design
  • Programming with Verilog, VHDL, and system scripting

Across all roles, you’ll solve technical challenges, work on impactful projects, and collaborate with teams across geographies and disciplines.

What Makes You Stand Out?

  • Strong programming (C/C++), scripting, and OOP skills (for SW roles)
  • Knowledge of networking, OS, or protocols (TCP/IP, LTE, SIP)
  • Analytical, logical reasoning, and a “learners’ mindset”
  • Clear, confident communication and thirst for teamwork

Career Advantages

  • Work on 5G, AI, mobile, and computing technologies
  • Mentorship from experts, exposure to global best practices and projects
  • Competitive CTC, high-impact projects, and learning opportunities
  • Grow your skills with one of the world’s best semiconductor innovators
