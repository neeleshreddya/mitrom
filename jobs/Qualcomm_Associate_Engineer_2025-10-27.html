Qualcomm – Associate Engineer Recruitment

Job Overview

Qualcomm is seeking motivated individuals for the position of Associate Engineer across multiple locations in India. This opportunity is open to recent graduates and early-career professionals with a background in Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical Engineering, or related disciplines. Successful applicants will contribute to cutting-edge semiconductor and wireless technology development in a collaborative, innovation-driven environment.

  • Position: Associate Engineer
  • Locations: Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Noida
  • Experience Level: Freshers (0–2 years)
  • Eligible Graduation Years: 2023, 2024, 2025
  • Application Deadline: Applications are considered on a rolling basis; apply promptly for best consideration.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform systematic data collection and analysis to support system testing.
  • Identify, investigate, and document system defects using established tracking tools.
  • Analyze logs to diagnose technical issues and collaborate on solutions.
  • Develop detailed technical models and translate design concepts into software and/or hardware solutions.
  • Create and execute comprehensive test plans, scenarios, and scripts for new and modified systems.
  • Implement and evaluate programs to assess the performance and reliability of engineering solutions.
  • Engage with managers and team members to resolve obstacles and deliver projects efficiently.
  • Escalate complex technical challenges to senior team members when necessary.

Areas of Focus

  • Physical Design for SoC and Hard Macro Components
  • System-on-Chip (SoC) Validation and Debugging
  • RF/Analog/Mixed-Signal Circuit and IC Design
  • Low-Power and Advanced Board/FPGA Design
  • Digital ASIC Architecture and Verification
  • CAD Engineering and Design-for-Test Solutions
  • CPU and Microarchitecture Design

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical/Electronic Engineering, or a related field.
  • Solid understanding of SoC architectures, including embedded RISC/DSP processors and communications/networking ASICs.
  • Proficiency in hardware description languages (Verilog, VHDL) and programming languages (C/C++, Tcl, Perl, shell-scripting).
  • Background in RTL design and/or object-oriented programming.
  • Knowledge of communications protocols and multimedia applications is advantageous.
  • Familiarity with analog/mixed-signal components and EDA tools (PLL, LNA, OpAmp, CMOS, ADC/DAC, Cadence, SpectreRF, Layout).
  • Strong analytical, troubleshooting, and communication skills.
  • Ability to collaborate effectively within engineering teams.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Background: Bachelor’s or Master’s (B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech/B.Sc/M.Sc) in relevant fields.
  • Graduation Batch: 2023, 2024, or 2025.
  • Experience: 0–2 years (ideal for recent graduates and freshers).
  • Technical Skills: As outlined above.
  • Teamwork and interpersonal capabilities.

Salary & Benefits

  • Compensation: ₹15 to ₹20 Lakh per annum (subject to experience and location), plus performance-linked incentives.
  • Benefits: Industry-leading employee welfare programs, professional growth opportunities, and a dynamic workplace culture.

Application Process

Interested candidates should prepare an up-to-date resume highlighting academic achievements, technical skills, and project experience relevant to this role. Applications will be considered as soon as received; early submission is encouraged to maximize consideration.

This opportunity provides an outstanding platform for entry-level engineers to advance their professional journey within the global leader in wireless technology and semiconductors.
