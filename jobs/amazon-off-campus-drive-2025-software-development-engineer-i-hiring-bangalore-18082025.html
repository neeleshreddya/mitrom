Amazon Software Development Engineer-I Recruitment 2025: Freshers Apply in Bengaluru

Overview of the Opportunity

Amazon’s engineering teams power some of the largest distributed systems and cloud platforms in the world. As an SDE-I in Bengaluru, you will be part of a collaborative group that builds efficient, scalable solutions for millions of customers. The work environment is fast-paced, supportive, and driven by a culture of invention.

Who Can Apply?

  • Degree Requirements: BE, BTech, ME, or MTech in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields.
  • Batch: 2025 graduates and current final-year students.
  • Experience Level: Entry-level role – fresh graduates are encouraged to apply.
  • Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.

Salary & Benefits

  • Package: Attractive compensation as per current industry benchmarks.
  • Work Perks: Mentorship, continuous learning, networking with top professionals, and exposure to next-gen technologies.

Amazon: Innovators at Heart

Amazon is renowned for its culture of customer focus, rapid innovation, and building reliable technology products. Software Development Engineers at Amazon work across cloud computing, AI, e-commerce, and digital infrastructure. You will collaborate with experts to shape technology that makes life easier for users around the globe.

What Will You Do?

  • Collaboration: Partner with senior engineers and cross-functional teams to design, code, and deliver quality products.
  • Technology Development: Build distributed systems, optimize performance, and maintain relevant technical documentation.
  • Problem Solving: Tackle diverse software challenges, from data management to scalable architectures.
  • Programming: Use languages like Java, C++, and Python to develop new applications and improve existing solutions.
  • Learning & Growth: Benefit from hands-on experience, workshops, and career advancement initiatives.

Qualifications & Preferred Skills

  • Basic: Enrolled in or recently completed a recognized engineering degree
  • Knowledge of core programming principles: algorithms, data structures, and object-oriented programming
  • Familiarity with at least one major programming language (Java, C++, Python)
  • Preferred: Previous internships or academic projects in tech
  • Understanding of distributed computing, databases, or optimization
  • Problem-solving mindset and ability to handle ambiguity

Inclusive Workplace

Amazon provides accommodations and support for candidates with disabilities throughout the application process. The company values diversity and offers an inclusive, equitable working environment.
