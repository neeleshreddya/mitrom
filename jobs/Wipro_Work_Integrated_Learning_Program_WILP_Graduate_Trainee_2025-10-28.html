Wipro Work Integrated Learning Program (WILP) – Graduate Trainee

Wipro is inviting applications for its Work Integrated Learning Program (WILP) for the roles of Graduate Trainee. This unique initiative offers B.Sc and BCA graduates from the 2024 and 2025 academic years the opportunity to gain industry experience while pursuing a fully sponsored M.Tech degree from a prestigious Indian institution. Selected candidates will engage in full-time employment at Wipro, receiving both professional training and academic advancement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Contribute to Wipro’s technology and business process services as an entry-level team member.
  • Participate in live projects and gain hands-on experience in IT, consulting, digital transformation, or analytics.
  • Balance on-the-job responsibilities with the academic commitments of the sponsored M.Tech program.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver solutions for domestic and international clients.
  • Demonstrate a commitment to both service excellence and academic achievement throughout the program.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Mathematics, Statistics, Electronics, Physics, or BCA.
  • Minimum of 60% aggregate or 6.0 CGPA as per university regulations.
  • Core Mathematics is required as a subject in graduation; Applied or Business Mathematics is not accepted.
  • Graduation must be completed within three years on a full-time basis.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of 10th and 12th grade from a recognized board. Distance or open education is permitted only for these levels.
  • Only one active backlog is permitted up to the time of interview; all backlogs must be cleared before onboarding.
  • A gap of up to three years is allowed between completion of 10th grade and the start of graduation, but no gaps are permitted during graduation.
  • Candidates must be at least 18 years old at the time of application.
  • Open to Indian nationals or holders of PIO/OCI cards; Bhutanese and Nepali nationals must provide a valid citizenship certificate.

Salary & Benefits

  • Monthly Stipend: ₹15,000 to ₹23,000 with annual increments.
  • Joining Bonus: ₹75,000, as detailed in the program policy.
  • Full sponsorship for M.Tech degree by Wipro.
  • Comprehensive training and career growth opportunities within Wipro.
  • Multicity work locations across India.
  • Service Agreement: Five years (60 months); early resignation may require return of joining bonus on a pro-rata basis.

Selection Process

  1. Online Assessment: Test of verbal, analytical, quantitative abilities, and written communication (80 minutes).
  2. Business Discussion: Evaluation of role suitability and technical understanding.
  3. HR Interview: General interview to assess soft skills and overall fit.
  4. Pre-skilling Training: Completion of preparatory modules as required.
  5. Final Offer: Issuance of the employment letter.

Wipro reserves the right to amend the selection procedure and enforce actions in case of any irregularities.

Application Process

Eligible candidates are required to submit their applications online before 31 October 2025 at 11:59 PM. All registration and selection parameters will be determined by Wipro’s hiring team.

This position offers a valuable pathway for fresh graduates aiming to build a robust IT career with a globally recognized technology leader, while advancing their qualifications through a fully supported M.Tech program.
