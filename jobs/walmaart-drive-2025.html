About Walmart

Walmart is a multinational retail corporation that operates a chain of hypermarkets, discount department stores, and grocery stores worldwide.

Job Overview

Walmart Global Tech India is hiring freshers for multiple software roles, offering an excellent opportunity to kickstart your career in one of the world’s top retailers.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain software applications.
  • Work with cross-functional teams to design and implement solutions.
  • Troubleshoot and debug issues.
  • Participate in code reviews and collaborate with team members.
  • Keep updated with emerging technologies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduates from 2023 to 2025 batches.
  • B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech in Computer Science or related fields.
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in academics.
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Knowledge of Python, Java, or C++ is a plus.
  • No active backlogs.

Selection Process

  1. Online aptitude and technical tests.
  2. Technical interviews.
  3. HR interview.
  4. Final offer and onboarding.

Salary & Benefits

Competitive salary packages with additional benefits and growth opportunities.
