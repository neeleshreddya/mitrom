Google Software Application Development Apprenticeship 2025: March Start – Apply Now!

Google is seeking enthusiastic graduates for its Software Application Development Apprenticeship, starting March 2026 across Bengaluru, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, and Mumbai. This unique program offers hands-on experience, technical training, and mentorship to help launch your career in software development.

Application Deadline: September 14, 2025
Stipend: ₹5 to ₹8 lakh per annum (estimated)
Program Duration: 12 months
Location Options: Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Gurgaon

About the Apprenticeship

The Google Apprenticeship blends formal classroom training with practical experience. You’ll spend part of your week in dedicated learning programs, while the remainder involves working on live projects with Google teams. Apprentices develop technical expertise in programming, coding standards, software testing, and project management, while also gaining exposure to collaborative team environments.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must hold a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or a related field, or possess equivalent practical experience.
  • Recent graduates are eligible; applicants should not have participated in any prior Government Apprenticeship schemes.
  • Aadhaar must be linked to a mobile number, and a personal email ID is mandatory.
  • Less than one year of programming experience (Java, C++, or Python).
  • Commitment to complete the program full-time in a hybrid office setup.
  • Must be ready to adhere to the program duration (18–24 weeks).

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience using Google Workspace or similar productivity suites.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and critical thinking.
  • Comfort with teamwork and handling multiple tasks independently.
  • Passion for technology and a proven track record of self-learning or personal projects.
  • Excellent communication, organization, and positive attitude.

About the Role

  • Learn and apply coding standards while supporting software development projects.
  • Participate in unit testing, integration, and troubleshooting tasks.
  • Gain hands-on experience on various aspects of the software lifecycle, from design to deployment.
  • Engage in discussions about real-world applications of software technologies.
  • Receive mentorship and feedback to further develop your technical skills.

This program is designed to give recent graduates and career changers the opportunity to gain professional software development experience and a completion certificate upon finishing.
