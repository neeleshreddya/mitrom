Overview

Salesforce, a global leader in CRM and cloud solutions, is offering an exciting Information Security Internship for final-year graduates in Computer Science or Information Technology. This internship is designed for students eager to build a career in cybersecurity and develop hands-on skills in a leading tech company.

Application Deadline: August 22, 2025
Location: Bangalore, India
Stipend: ₹8.4 LPA (as per Glassdoor estimates)

Who Should Apply?

  • Final-year BE/B.Tech students in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Fresh graduates interested in cybersecurity and technology compliance careers.
  • Individuals motivated to join a global tech company and learn the latest in security standards.

Position Highlights

  • Job Role: Information Security Intern
  • Education: Bachelor’s Degree (final year or fresh graduate)
  • Experience: 0–3 years (freshers welcome)
  • Category: Fixed Term, Temporary Internship

About Salesforce

Salesforce stands as the Customer Company, pioneering the future of business with powerful AI, advanced data platforms, and CRM expertise. Working at Salesforce means driving innovation, embracing global collaboration, and making a positive impact on clients and communities.

Responsibility Summary

  • Complete security and compliance questionnaires with Salesforce customers and internal teams.
  • Maintain customer-facing documents, white papers, and compliance resources.
  • Organize and support global security audits, including evidence gathering and documentation.
  • Contribute feedback to product management for enhanced security features.
  • Build and maintain internal security knowledge bases and training resources for Sales and Customer Success teams.
  • Prepare for audits and stay ahead of compliance requirements using Salesforce (SFDC) platform.
  • Collaborate independently and within teams to achieve shared objectives.

Desired Experience and Skills

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Security, or technology-related field
  • Postgraduate or formal training in cybersecurity (plus)
  • Interest or experience in security assessments, RFP/RFI responses, and cloud security questionnaires
  • Familiarity with major security/cloud certifications (CSA CAIQ, SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS)
  • Excellent research, analytical, and communication skills
  • Ability to meet deadlines and handle multiple tasks

Note: Other duties as needed for the internship and organizational goals.
