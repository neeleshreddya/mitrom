Capgemini Off Campus Drive 2025 – Hiring Network Engineer

Capgemini has announced its Off Campus Drive 2025 to hire Network Engineers for the Gurgaon location. This opportunity is open exclusively to B.Tech/BE (CSIT/Circuit branches) and MCA graduates from the 2025 batch. Freshers who meet the eligibility criteria can apply before the deadline.

Job Overview – Capgemini Off Campus Drive 2025

  • Company: Capgemini
  • Role: Network Engineer
  • Qualification: B.Tech/BE (CSIT/Circuit branches only) / MCA
  • Experience: Freshers (2025 batch)
  • Location: Gurgaon
  • Salary: ₹4.25 LPA
  • Last Date to Apply: 18 August 2025, 11:59 PM

Job Description

The Network Engineer role at Capgemini involves working on real-time networking technologies and supporting clients with high-quality solutions. Candidates should have a strong interest in learning and applying new technologies in networking, security, and cloud systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Troubleshoot and resolve network-related issues.
  • Participate in projects and contribute as a team member.
  • Seek guidance when needed to ensure quality delivery.
  • Continuously improve technical knowledge and skills.
  • Demonstrate problem-solving ability and a customer-first approach.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech/BE in CSIT or Circuit branches, or MCA (2025 pass-outs only).
  • Good understanding of networking concepts: routing, switching, collaboration, data center, wireless, and security.
  • CCNA certification is preferred.
  • Strong communication skills (written and verbal).
  • Positive learning attitude and ability to work in a team.

Additional Information

  • This role is open only for Gurgaon location.
  • Candidates must be ready to work in rotational shifts (early morning, evening, or night). No general day shifts will be provided.
  • Only shortlisted candidates will be invited for the online assessments and interviews.
  • Applicants must ensure a stable internet connection and laptop/desktop with camera and audio for the virtual process.

Selection Process

  1. Online Assessment – Aptitude and technical basics.
  2. Technical Interview – Networking fundamentals, certifications, and problem-solving.
  3. HR Interview – Communication, flexibility, and cultural fit.

Why Join Capgemini?

  • Global exposure and career development opportunities.
  • Hands-on experience with networking and cloud-based technologies.
  • Inclusive workplace with diversity initiatives.
  • Structured learning and mentoring programs.

This Off Campus Drive by Capgemini is an excellent opportunity for fresh graduates from the 2025 batch who are looking to kick-start their career in network engineering. Interested candidates are advised to prepare well for the assessments and interviews to secure this role.
