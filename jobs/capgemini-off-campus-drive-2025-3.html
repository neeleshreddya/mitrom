About Capgemini

Capgemini is a global leader in consulting, digital transformation, and technology. With operations in over 40 countries, Capgemini combines deep sector expertise with the latest digital innovations, helping clients reshape their businesses for a connected future.

Job Details

  • Role: Contact Support Group (CSG) – IT Enabler
  • Qualification: Any 3-year graduate or diploma (2025 batch)
  • Location: Noida
  • Salary: ₹3.25 Lacs
  • Deadline: 17 August 2025, 11:59 PM

Job Overview

Capgemini Exceller is looking for enthusiastic fresher (2025 batch) graduates and diploma holders to join their Contact Support Group team in Noida. As part of the CSG team, you will play a vital role in supporting IT infrastructure and delivering top-notch customer service to global clients.

Responsibilities

  • Handle IT support requests via phone, chat, email, and digital channels.
  • Manage and resolve tickets using business intelligence tools.
  • Deliver customer support in a 24/7 environment and work with global teams.
  • Communicate clearly, empathize, and deliver solutions within agreed SLAs.
  • Update knowledge base and collaborate with AI-based assistance platforms.
  • Promote customer engagement and improve service using digital tools.

Who Should Apply?

  • 2025 graduates or diploma holders with strong communication skills.
  • Ability to multitask and perform under pressure.
  • Basic IT and computing knowledge.
  • Willingness to work in rotational night/day shifts.
  • Female candidates are especially encouraged as part of diversity hiring.
  • Must have DigiLocker and Aadhar-linked mobile number.

Selection Process

  1. Shortlisting (based on eligibility and application)
  2. Online assessment (technical and aptitude)
  3. Technical Interviews
  4. Virtual HR interview
  5. Offer & onboarding

Important Notes

  • Only shortlisted candidates will receive further communication.
  • Entire process will be virtual; candidates are responsible for setup.
  • Indian citizens only. Participation does not guarantee selection.

Perks & Benefits

  • Competitive salary and health benefits
  • Professional growth and learning programs
  • Work in a collaborative, future-focused environment
