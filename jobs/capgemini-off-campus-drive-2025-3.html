About Capgemini

Capgemini is hiring freshers for the Contact Support Group (CSG) role at their Noida location. This opportunity is open for 2025 batch graduates or diploma holders.

Job Details

  • Role: Contact Support Group (CSG)
  • Qualification: Any 3-year graduate/diploma
  • Experience: Freshers
  • Batch: 2025
  • Salary: ₹3.25 LPA
  • Job Location: Noida
  • Application Deadline: 17 August 2025

Key Requirements

  • Active DigiLocker account and a mobile number linked with Aadhar.
  • Applications from female candidates are particularly encouraged as part of diversity initiatives.

About the Role

  • Providing technical troubleshooting and problem resolution to customer queries.
  • Managing tickets efficiently and keeping customers informed.
  • Using business intelligence tools to optimize support processes.
  • Delivering exceptional customer service across telecom, finance, healthcare, and technology industries.
  • Assisting customers with internet troubleshooting, password resets, and profile updates.
  • Working in 24/7 rotational shifts, interacting with clients across different regions and time zones.

Desired Skills

  • Excellent communication and English-speaking skills.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work under pressure.
  • Basic computing knowledge.
  • Willingness to work in a 24/7 environment and relocate if needed.
  • Ability to sign Service Level Agreements (SLA) and meet KPIs.
