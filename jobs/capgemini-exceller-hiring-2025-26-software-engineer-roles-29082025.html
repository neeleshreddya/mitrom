Capgemini Exceller 2025-26: Software Engineer Recruitment for Fresh Graduates

Join Telegram Join WhatsApp Instagram

Capgemini, a renowned worldwide provider of IT consulting, digital, and cloud services, has announced its Exceller 2025-26 Hiring Program — a unique career launchpad for India’s new generation of engineering graduates. This drive aims to recruit outstanding freshers for key Software Engineer roles across PAN India, shaping the next wave of technology talent.

About the Program

Capgemini's Exceller Hiring Program is designed to build teams who drive digital transformation, cloud migration, business analytics, and automation for global clients. If you are passionate about learning, collaborating, and working on real-world enterprise projects, the Exceller program provides coaching, continuous upskilling, project rotations, and a supportive environment. As part of Capgemini, you get to solve some of the toughest technology challenges in business today.

Position Details

  • Roles: Software Engineer (Analyst, Analyst*, Senior Analyst)
  • Salary: Analyst: ₹4.25 LPA, Analyst*: ₹5.75 LPA, Senior Analyst: ₹7.5 LPA
  • Eligibility: BE/BTech/ME/MTech (Any Specialisation); Batch: 2025, 2026, 2027
  • Locations: PAN India (placement based on business needs and candidate performance)

Eligibility Criteria

  • At least 60% aggregate in Diploma, Graduation (up to 7th semester), and ME/MTech (up to 5th semester)
  • No active backlogs as of the application date
  • Maximum 1-year gap allowed between academic milestones (X, XII, graduation, post-graduation)
  • No gaps are allowed within a degree phase (ex: completing BE/BTech in more than 4 years disqualifies)
  • Open only to students from colleges registered with Capgemini’s campus recruitment drive

How to Apply for Capgemini Exceller 2025-26

  • Apply via the Superset platform (link only accessible to students from eligible colleges)
  • Applicants must have a DigiLocker account and Aadhaar-linked mobile number for verification
  • Application deadline: 5 September 2025. Coordinate with your placement cell for access.

Key Advantages of Joining Capgemini

  • Be part of one of the world’s top IT employers — stable, diverse, and global
  • Gain real project experience and exposure to in-demand digital skills
  • Attractive salaries and clear career progression paths with annual performance appraisals
  • Access to a vast learning ecosystem and professional certifications
  • Mentorship, supportive teams, and a culture of continuous improvement

More Opportunities

If you’re exploring other pathways, check out current openings for software engineers and analysts at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Philips, Shell, Google, and other top employers. You can filter by job location (Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Noida, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurgaon), by batch (2018 — 2027), or respective degree or stream.
