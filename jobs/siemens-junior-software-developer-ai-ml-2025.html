About Simens

Simens is a global leader in digital payments and financial technology, providing secure, reliable solutions that empower businesses and consumers across over 200 countries. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, Simens enables seamless payment experiences worldwide.

Job Overview

Simens is seeking passionate Software Test Engineers to join their technology team based in Bengaluru. This opportunity is open for fresh graduates from the 2023 and 2024 batches who want to build their careers working with automation frameworks, API testing, and modern cloud technologies.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Work collaboratively with Agile teams to ensure software quality and performance standards.
  • Design, develop, and maintain quality assurance frameworks and test automation scripts.
  • Conduct API testing, validate CI/CD pipelines, and perform cloud-native system verifications.
  • Participate actively in building scalable, efficient, and secure software applications.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E/B.Tech degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related technical fields.
  • Applicants must be freshers from the 2023 or 2024 batches.
  • Basic knowledge of Java, API testing tools, and cloud platforms is preferred.
  • Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving capabilities.

Selection Process

  1. Online assessment covering technical skills and aptitude.
  2. Technical interviews to assess domain knowledge and problem-solving ability.
  3. HR interview to understand cultural fit and communication skills.
  4. Final offer and onboarding for selected candidates.

Salary & Benefits

Simens offers competitive salary packages along with health benefits, learning opportunities, and exposure to a global fintech environment.
