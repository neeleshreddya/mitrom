Role Summary

As a Graduate Trainee Engineer within the AI/ML domain, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams on the design, development, and deployment of intelligent software systems. You'll support automation frameworks, data pipelines, and model implementations, contributing to real-world applications that power Siemens' technological edge.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain clean, scalable code for AI/ML applications.
  • Assist in building data workflows and experiment with model training processes.
  • Participate in code reviews, automated testing, and CI/CD pipelines.
  • Work alongside product teams, QA, and DevOps to ensure seamless product delivery.
  • Document technical workflows and contribute to knowledge sharing.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering (Computer Science, Information Technology, Electrical, Electronics, or related fields).
  • Strong coding skills in Python, Java, or C++.
  • Clear understanding of data structures, algorithms, and basic software engineering principles.
  • Familiarity with Git and CI/CD practices.
  • Good verbal and written communication skills.

Selection Process

  1. Fill out the online application form and submit your resume.
  2. Clear the technical & aptitude assessment.
  3. Attend one or more technical interviews.
  4. Participate in the HR interview.
  5. Receive the final offer letter, if selected.

Salary & Benefits

Competitive salary packages, comprehensive health benefits, professional development opportunities, and the chance to work with cutting-edge AI/ML technologies in a global innovation environment.
