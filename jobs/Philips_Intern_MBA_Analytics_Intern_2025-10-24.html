Philips: MBA Analytics Internship Opportunity 2025

Philips is seeking motivated candidates for the role of MBA Analytics Intern at its Bangalore location. This internship provides a platform for emerging professionals to develop analytical expertise and contribute to business-critical projects within a global leader in health technology.

Key Responsibilities

  • Gather and Interpret Requirements: Translate business needs into actionable data analytics initiatives and identify essential KPIs.
  • Analyze Diverse Data Sets: Collect, cleanse, and interpret data from multiple sources to uncover trends and insights using statistical methods.
  • Develop Analytical Solutions: Collaborate with various departments to conceptualize and implement analytics solutions—ensuring technical feasibility and validity.
  • Support Project Management: Engage with stakeholders in planning, risk management, and timely delivery of key initiatives.
  • Promote Process Improvement: Lead ongoing efforts to standardize, automate, and enhance data-related workflows for greater organizational efficiency.

Required Qualifications

  • Educational Background: MBA in Analytics from a recognized top-tier management institution.
  • Work Experience: Open to candidates with 0–3 years of experience.
  • Technical Proficiency:
    • Experience or strong understanding in finance, procurement, or supply chain domains (required).
    • Awareness of analytics technologies—data visualization, data engineering, and data science principles.
    • Hands-on skills with at least some of:
      • Productivity Tools: MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio, Outlook
      • Business Intelligence Platforms: Power BI, QlikSense, QlikView, Tableau, Power Automate
      • Programming Languages: Python, R
      • Data Management: SAP (required), SharePoint (required), SQL Server, Oracle
      • Familiarity with Data Science and Machine Learning concepts
    • Process Awareness: Acquainted with agile project methodologies and vendor/stakeholder negotiation techniques.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Strong academic record in business analytics.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and organizational skills.
  • Demonstrated attention to detail for data accuracy.
  • Ability to thrive in multicultural, virtual environments and manage cross-functional coordination.
  • Energetic, resourceful, adaptable, and capable of managing multiple priorities.

Preferred Certifications

  • Relevant credentials in data visualization or analytics tools.
  • Certification in Agile Project Management.

Core Competencies

  • Customer Orientation: Commit to understanding and exceeding client needs.
  • Integrity & Quality Focus: Consistently uphold high standards for data integrity and patient safety.
  • Collaborative Mindset: Work constructively across teams and hierarchical levels.
  • Ownership & Initiative: Take proactive responsibility for achieving objectives.
  • Continuous Improvement: Apply Lean and process optimization methodologies to drive excellence.

Salary & Benefits

  • Compensation: Up to ₹9 lakhs per annum (CTC, expected)
  • Location: Bangalore, India

Application Process

  • Deadline: Submit your application by September 29, 2025
  • Prepare a comprehensive resume highlighting qualifications, relevant experience, and technical skills.
  • Qualified applicants will be contacted for further assessments.

This role offers valuable exposure to business analytics with real-world impact, excellent learning opportunities, and professional development within a renowned multinational environment.
