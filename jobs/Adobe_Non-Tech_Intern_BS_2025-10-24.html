Adobe Non-Tech Intern BS – Noida

Job Overview

Adobe is seeking motivated individuals for the Non-Tech Intern BS position, ideal for recent graduates interested in contributing to world-class digital experiences. Interns will join a culture focused on innovation, inclusivity, and personal growth, working alongside talented professionals in a dynamic corporate environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with a range of non-technical projects supporting Adobe’s mission to deliver exceptional digital experiences.
  • Collaborate with colleagues across departments including sales, marketing, and engineering.
  • Support ongoing initiatives with analytical insights and effective teamwork.
  • Take part in team meetings, contribute ideas, and engage in problem-solving activities.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree (recently completed).
  • No prior professional experience required.
  • Demonstrated effective communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to think analytically and adapt to changing project needs.
  • Commitment to working collaboratively in a diverse, fast-paced team setting.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Recent graduate eligible for full-time employment.
  • Willingness to work onsite in Noida.
  • Eager to participate in cross-functional assignments and open to new learning opportunities.

Salary & Benefits

  • Competitive compensation: Expected salary is ₹14.9 LPA.
  • Gain exposure to vital business functions and develop professionally.
  • Experience a supportive workplace that values equal opportunity, continuous feedback, and flexible work policies.

Application Process

  1. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis; interested candidates are encouraged to apply promptly for best consideration.
  2. No specific closing date; early applicants may receive priority.
