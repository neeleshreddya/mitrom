Internship Opening: Data Analytics & Automation — Rolls-Royce (Pune)

Job Overview

Rolls-Royce is inviting applications for the role of Intern – Data Analytics & Automation at its Pune office, specifically for students from the 2025 graduating class. This internship is tailored for individuals pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s in Engineering—specializations accepted include Mechanical, Computer Science, Electrical, or Electronics. The program provides an opportunity to contribute to advanced analytical and automation projects within a global engineering organization renowned for its innovation and industry leadership.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct comprehensive literature reviews to inform data-driven solutions.
  • Develop, implement, and optimize analytical models and automation tools to enhance processes in areas such as Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul, Maintenance Scheduling, Troubleshooting, and Equipment Health Monitoring.
  • Design interactive dashboards and visualizations to facilitate actionable insights for engineering teams.
  • Accurately document all project-related activities and outcomes to ensure transparency and reproducibility.

Required Qualifications

  • Actively enrolled in a Bachelor’s or Master’s program in Engineering (Mechanical, Computer Science, Electrical, or Electronics).
  • Demonstrated proficiency in programming, with experience in analytics or machine learning methodologies.
  • Hands-on familiarity with data and analytics platforms and languages, including Azure Databricks, R, Python, PySpark, Hive, Hadoop, SQL, Tableau, or Power BI.
  • Strong ability to operate in agile, collaborative environments; highly motivated, proactive, and persistent.
  • Exceptional logical reasoning, analytical thinking, and creativity for tackling technical problems.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must currently be enrolled in a relevant engineering degree program (Bachelor’s or Master’s).
  • Graduation should be scheduled for 2025.
  • Only applicants without prior full-time work experience are eligible for this opportunity.

Salary & Benefits

  • Compensation: Expected salary range is ₹3 to ₹4 lakhs per annum.
  • Location: Pune, Maharashtra.
  • Opportunity to learn from leading experts in digital transformation and automation within a renowned multinational enterprise.

Application Process

  1. Interested candidates should apply as soon as possible; applications will be considered on a rolling basis until the vacancy is filled.
  2. Ensure your CV and academic credentials are up-to-date and relevant to the data analytics and automation field.

This internship offers an immersive experience in cutting-edge analytics and automation, providing a foundation for a successful engineering career within one of the world’s leading technology enterprises.
