Salesforce Off-Campus Drive 2025 – Software Engineering AMTS

Salesforce, the world leader in AI-powered CRM, is conducting its Off-Campus Drive 2025 to hire freshers for the Software Engineering AMTS role. This is an excellent opportunity for passionate and skilled graduates to start their career with a globally renowned organization and work on cutting-edge technologies.

Job Location

  • Bangalore
  • Hyderabad

Eligibility Criteria

  • BE / B.Tech graduates (2026 batch) in Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, or equivalent fields with a specialization in computer science.
  • Minimum CGPA of 7 with no active backlogs.
  • Freshers only.

Expected Salary/CTC

₹15 to 36 LPA (based on performance and role).

Application Deadline

12 August 2025 (only a few days left to apply!)

About Salesforce & Futureforce University Recruiting

Salesforce is a global AI CRM platform where innovation meets impact. The Futureforce University Recruiting Program is dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and retaining talented graduates. Through this program:

  • Graduates work on real-life projects that influence Salesforce’s global operations.
  • Participants receive mentorship, training programs, and exposure to industry best practices.
  • The program promotes collaboration across teams worldwide, giving candidates the chance to work in a truly global environment.
  • At Salesforce, ambition meets action. By joining, you become part of an organization that combines technology, innovation, and human-centric solutions to drive business success.

Job Description – Software Engineering AMTS

  • Architect, design, implement, test, and deliver highly scalable products.
  • Master Salesforce’s development process, culture, and codebase, and work towards improving them.
  • Collaborate with engineers, product managers, UX experts, and performance engineers to design features that delight customers.
  • Develop test strategies, automation frameworks, and write unit/functional tests to ensure high-quality software.
  • Analyze, design, and implement automated test suites to improve efficiency and maintainability.

This role is perfect for candidates who are passionate about software development, problem-solving, and creating innovative solutions that make a tangible difference.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Provide end-to-end solutions while ensuring high-quality and scalable code.
  2. Participate in the full software development lifecycle – planning, coding, testing, and deployment.
  3. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design innovative software solutions.
  4. Assist in automating testing processes to improve efficiency and reliability.
  5. Analyze metrics and telemetry data to drive operational excellence.
  6. Understand customer needs and help enhance Salesforce solutions globally.

Desired Skills

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • Excellent teamwork and collaboration skills.
  • Self-motivated, proactive, and eager to learn new technologies.
  • Ability to adapt in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.

Why Join Salesforce?

  • Work in a global, innovative environment with cutting-edge AI technologies.
  • Gain exposure to real-time projects impacting millions of users worldwide.
  • Access to mentorship programs, skill development courses, and networking opportunities.
  • Opportunity to work with the best minds in the industry and grow professionally.
  • Become part of an organization that values diversity, inclusion, and sustainable innovation.
