MBA Analytics Intern – Philips

Job Overview

Philips is seeking a highly motivated MBA Analytics Intern to contribute to business growth by delivering actionable insights through data-driven analysis. This internship provides hands-on experience in data analytics, project management, and stakeholder collaboration, supporting critical initiatives across multiple business domains.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify and translate organizational requirements into relevant analytics objectives and measurable KPIs.
  • Collect, clean, and analyze datasets from various platforms to uncover business trends using statistical and data science techniques.
  • Develop and implement analytics solutions in partnership with cross-functional teams to address strategic and operational needs.
  • Support project planning activities, assess risks, and monitor progress to ensure timely execution of deliverables.
  • Promote continuous improvement through standardization, automation, and process optimization to enhance functional efficiency.
  • Prepare comprehensive reports and presentations to communicate findings and recommendations to internal stakeholders.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree (MBA in Analytics from a leading institution preferred).
  • 0–3 years’ professional experience; recent graduates and freshers are encouraged to apply.
  • Demonstrated understanding of finance, procurement, or supply chain processes.
  • Familiarity with modern analytics tools and technologies, including data visualization, data engineering, and data science.
  • Experience using BI tools such as Power BI, QlikSense, QlikView, Tableau, and Power Automate.
  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Visio, Outlook).
  • Working knowledge of programming languages including Python and R.
  • Experience connecting and analyzing data sources such as SAP, SharePoint (mandatory), SQL Server, and Oracle.
  • Strong foundation in data science and machine learning concepts.
  • Certifications in data visualization and agile project management are considered an advantage.
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills, attention to detail, and ability to manage multiple priorities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pursuing or completed a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree, preferably with an MBA in Analytics.
  • Relevant academic projects or internships in finance, procurement, supply chain, or analytics.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and coordination skills.
  • Exposure to multicultural and remote work environments is beneficial.

Salary & Benefits

  • Expected compensation up to ₹9 LPA for selected candidates.
  • Typical internship stipend ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹40,000 per month.
  • Located primarily in Bangalore; opportunities may also be available in Pune and for remote work, depending on business needs.

Application Process

  • Submit your application by September 29, 2025.
  • Ensure your resume outlines specific skills, relevant experience, and educational qualifications.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for further selection steps.
