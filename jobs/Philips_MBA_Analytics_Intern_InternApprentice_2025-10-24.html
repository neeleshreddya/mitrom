Philips - MBA Analytics Intern (Intern/Apprentice)

MBA Analytics Intern

Company: Philips
Location: Bangalore
Application Deadline: September 29, 2025

Job Overview

Philips is seeking a highly motivated MBA Analytics Intern to support data-driven decision-making and contribute to operational excellence. The internship focuses on extracting meaningful business insights through advanced analytics, collaboration, and project management.

Key Responsibilities

  • Requirement Identification: Interpret complex business needs and translate them into actionable data analysis initiatives.
  • Data Collection & Analysis: Gather information from multiple sources, maintain data accuracy, and apply statistical techniques to uncover significant trends and patterns.
  • Analytics Solution Development: Work with various stakeholders to design and evaluate solutions that address business challenges. Participate in feasibility assessments and support solution implementation through validation.
  • Project Coordination: Assist in project planning, assess potential risks, and aim to ensure timely and accurate project completion.
  • Process Enhancement: Promote continuous improvement by standardizing workflows, simplifying tasks, and automating processes for increased efficiency.

Required Qualifications

  • Educational Background:
    • MBA specialization in Analytics from a premier management institution
    • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in a relevant discipline
  • Experience:
    • 0 to 3 years in analytics or related fields
  • Domain Knowledge:
    • Proficiency in finance, procurement, or supply chain is essential
  • Technical Expertise:
    • Familiarity with business intelligence tools (Power BI, Qliksense, Qlikview, Tableau, Power Automate)
    • Programming skills in Python and R
    • Experience with data sources such as SAP, SharePoint (required), SQL Server, Oracle
    • Skills in data visualization, data engineering, and data science
  • Project Methodology:
    • Practical understanding of Agile software development lifecycle
    • Ability to engage and negotiate with diverse stakeholders

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must possess strong communication and presentation skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to interact effectively within cross-functional and multicultural environments.
  • Capacity to multitask, adapt to change, and manage stakeholder expectations.
  • Motivated, energetic, and resourceful personality.
  • Preference given to individuals holding certifications in data visualization, data management tools, or Agile project management.

Salary & Benefits

  • Compensation: Up to ₹9 Lakhs Per Annum (LPA)
  • Exposure to industry-leading practices in analytics and healthcare technology
  • Opportunity to collaborate with multifunctional teams in a dynamic setting

Application Process

Interested candidates meeting the qualifications should submit their application before the deadline: September 29, 2025.

Competencies required include:

  • Commitment to customer-centric solutions
  • Strong sense of integrity and attention to detail
  • Effective team collaboration
  • Accountability for outcomes and drive to achieve results quickly
  • Continuous improvement mindset
