About Visa

Visa is a global leader in digital payments, enabling secure transactions in more than 200 countries. Through innovation and advanced technology, Visa connects consumers, businesses, and governments, ensuring seamless and reliable payment experiences worldwide.

Job Overview

Visa is hiring Software Test Engineers for its technology team in Bengaluru. This role offers fresh graduates from 2023 and 2024 batches the chance to work on cutting-edge payment technologies, automation frameworks, and scalable systems.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with Agile teams to ensure product quality and performance.
  • Design, develop, and maintain QA frameworks and automation scripts.
  • Perform API testing, CI/CD pipeline validation, and cloud-native verification.
  • Contribute to building efficient, scalable, and secure software solutions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E/B.Tech in Computer Science, IT, or related fields.
  • Freshers from 2023 or 2024 batch.
  • Basic knowledge of Java, API testing, and cloud platforms preferred.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.

Selection Process

  1. Online assessment (technical + aptitude)
  2. Technical interview(s)
  3. HR interview
  4. Final offer & onboarding

Salary & Benefits

Competitive salary packages, health benefits, learning opportunities, and global exposure within the fintech industry.
