Philips Internship 2025: Paid Internship for Engineering and Science Graduates – Apply Now!

Join Telegram Join WhatsApp Instagram

Philips, a leader in health technology and innovation, has opened applications for its 2025 Internship Program. This program offers highly motivated students and recent graduates a real opportunity to contribute to innovation and technology at the company’s Pune R&D center.

Opportunity Overview

  • Position: Intern
  • Qualification: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Engineering or Science (recent batches)
  • Location: Pune
  • Stipend: Up to ₹9 LPA (expected)
  • Field: Biomedical, Mechanical, Electronics, Software, Mechatronics, Data Science, Embedded, and related areas
  • Application Deadline: ASAP

Internship Responsibilities

  • Detailing out the project scope assigned by Philips DXR R&D
  • Learning the product, process, and tools associated with the project
  • Translating user and business requirements into product requirements and high-level design
  • Gaining exposure to Model-Based System Engineering, System Integration, Automation, and more
  • Participating in regular project reviews and ensuring timely completion of milestones

Skills and Experience Needed

  • Strong foundation in fields like biomedical, electronics, mechanical, software, mechatronics, data science, or embedded systems
  • Ability to learn quickly and show curiosity for new technologies and business systems
  • Excellent communication skills and comfort with presenting ideas to teams
  • Self-organized and structured work habits
  • Mindset focused on simplicity, speed, and practical solutions

Program Benefits

  • Work with high-end technologies, tools, and infrastructure in a leading research and development setting
  • Interaction with domain experts and senior technology professionals
  • Opportunity for rich industry experience

How to Apply

Interested and eligible candidates can apply online using the official Philips careers link below. Early applications are encouraged due to limited internship slots. Ensure your resume is updated to maximize shortlisting chances.
