Research Intern – AI / Platform for AI / Quantum

Company: IBM Research India

Locations: Bangalore, Gurgaon

Internship Duration: May 2026 – August 2026 (3 Months)

Stipend: ₹42,343 per month (Estimated)

Application Deadline: Open until filled

Eligible Batch: 2026

Experience Level: Students / Fresh Graduates

Job Overview

IBM Research India is seeking highly motivated students for internship roles in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Platform for AI, and Quantum Computing. This program offers the opportunity to engage in innovative research projects, collaborate with leading experts, and gain practical experience in cutting-edge technologies. The internship also serves as a pathway for potential full-time employment.

Key Responsibilities

For AI Research Interns

  • Collaborate on real-time research initiatives in AI, hybrid cloud, and quantum computing.
  • Develop and execute code using Python and leading data science libraries (e.g., Pandas, NumPy).
  • Work extensively with deep learning architectures, foundation models, and large language models.
  • Leverage generative AI frameworks such as LangChain and CrewAI.
  • Utilize tools including VS Code, Jupyter Notebooks, and Git for development and collaboration.
  • Analyze and process large datasets for AI-driven solutions.
  • Conduct research on enterprise LLM applications, AI automation, middleware, information retrieval, foundation models (multimodal and time series), GenAI security, AI-enabled mathematics, and reasoning.

For Platform for AI Research Interns

  • Train, finetune, and perform inference with advanced language models.
  • Build prototypes to solve real-world business challenges.
  • Partner with infrastructure teams and research scientists.
  • Apply expertise in Java/Python, data structures, and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, IBM Cloud).
  • Utilize container platforms such as Docker, Kubernetes, or OpenShift.
  • Contribute to distributed model training using frameworks like PyTorch and FSDP, including GPU and hardware optimization.
  • Explore dense, MoE, Mamba, and multimodal architectures.

For Quantum Research Interns

  • Participate in ongoing quantum computing research projects.
  • Develop applications with Java/Python and Qiskit; Rust programming is advantageous.
  • Apply foundational knowledge of quantum computing, graph theory, and probability.
  • Explore agentic AI, deep reinforcement learning, and quantum algorithm design (e.g., QAOA).
  • Gain exposure to tensor networks, Kubernetes, CI/CD, GitOps, and open source software development.

Required Qualifications

  • Current enrolment in B.E., B.Tech, M.E., M.Tech (including dual degree), or Ph.D. program.
  • Achieved a minimum score of 70% or a CGPA of at least 7.0 in the ongoing degree.
  • Proficient coding and analytical thinking abilities.
  • For AI Intern positions: Solid grounding in AI/ML techniques, deep learning principles, data handling, and experience with frameworks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.
  • For Platform for AI/Quantum Intern roles: Additional expertise in cloud technologies, infrastructure, Qiskit, Rust, and experience with open source or foundation model training.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to students graduating in 2026.
  • Candidates from engineering and technology disciplines (undergraduate or postgraduate) are encouraged.
  • Individuals with a demonstrated interest in research and innovative problem-solving are preferred.

Preferred Skills

  • Practical knowledge of containerization tools (Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift).
  • Awareness of AI system risks, such as agent safety and AI governance.
  • Familiarity with distributed computing, GPU hardware, and advanced data science tools.

Salary & Benefits

  • Monthly stipend estimated at ₹42,343.
  • Gain hands-on experience working on real-world problems in a world-renowned research environment.
  • Access mentorship from leading researchers and networking opportunities.
  • Potential pathway to future full-time roles based on performance.

Application Process

Interested candidates should submit their applications via IBM’s official careers portal. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis; early submission is strongly encouraged.
