Okta Off Campus Drive 2025: Associate Technical Support Engineer – Freshers Apply Now!

Join Telegram Join WhatsApp Instagram

Are you a recent Computer Science graduate aspiring to launch your career at a global leader in identity and access management? Okta’s Off Campus Drive 2025 presents a fantastic opportunity for fresh talent to join their team as an Associate Technical Support Engineer in Bangalore. This role offers continuous learning, mentorship, and the chance to make a real impact supporting enterprise customers.

About the Position

  • Role: New Grad – Associate Technical Support Engineer
  • Location: Bangalore
  • Salary: Expected ₹3–6 LPA
  • Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field; recent graduates preferred

What You’ll Do

  • Primary Customer Support Contact: Act as the main point of contact for Okta’s enterprise customers, providing technical support and guidance within published SLAs.
  • Troubleshoot and Resolve: Investigate and resolve complex technical issues related to Okta’s products via phone, email, and ticketing systems.
  • Timely Communication: Ensure ongoing, clear communication with customers about issue status and progress.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Document solutions, contribute to Okta’s internal knowledge base, and help streamline support for teammates and future customers.
  • Collaboration: Work in close partnership with senior engineers, the product team, and other technical groups to address multifaceted challenges quickly.
  • Continuous Learning: Participate in training programs to stay sharp on Okta product updates and the latest trends in identity, security, and access management.

Required Skills and Knowledge

  • Solid understanding of computer science fundamentals, including cyber security, cryptography, and OSI networking protocols (SMTP, DNS, DHCP)
  • Familiarity with multi-factor authentication (MFA) mechanisms and cross-platform operating systems (Linux, Mac OS X, Windows)
  • Strong analytical, organizational, and communication abilities
  • Ability to remain calm and articulate in high-pressure customer interactions
  • Quick learner; eager to tackle new technical tools and concepts

Benefits and Growth

  • Real-world impact supporting enterprise customers
  • Career mentorship and learning pathways
  • Inclusive workplace culture and a collaborative support community
  • Flexible work policies and social impact opportunities
  • Enjoyable perks in a fast-paced environment

How to Apply

Eligible candidates can submit their applications online. Make sure you provide up-to-date academic and contact details. Selection is rolling, so apply as soon as possible to maximize your chances.
