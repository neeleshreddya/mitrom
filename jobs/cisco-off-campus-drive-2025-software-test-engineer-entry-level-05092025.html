Cisco Off Campus Drive 2025: Software Test Engineer (Entry Level) – Bangalore

Join Telegram Join WhatsApp Instagram

Cisco, a world leader in networking and digital transformation, is inviting applications from recent engineering graduates for their Software Test Engineer (Entry Level) position. Freshers and early-career engineers have the opportunity to join a global team, work on advanced technology, and jumpstart their professional journey.

Role Details

  • Position: Software Test Engineer (Entry Level)
  • Eligibility: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or Engineering
  • Experience: Freshers & early career engineers (up to 2 years via study, projects, or internships)
  • Location: Bangalore
  • Salary Range: ₹4.2 – ₹6 LPA (Glassdoor estimate)
  • Application Deadline: ASAP

What You’ll Do

  • Develop software tests focused on wireless solutions, automation, networking, and tooling
  • Build and execute tests for network operating systems and wireless devices
  • Work alongside global teams on technology powering critical applications
  • Engage in continuous learning, including emerging areas like artificial intelligence

As a Software Test & Automation Engineer at Cisco, you’ll participate in the full development lifecycle of large-scale wireless technologies. You’ll be part of a diverse team working collaboratively at the intersection of physical and digital, with a direct impact on real-world connectivity experiences for customers worldwide.

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in writing software in C, C++, or Python
  • Knowledge of networking fundamentals: TCP/IP, protocols
  • Ability to quickly learn new tools and technologies
  • Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills
  • Interest in software quality assurance and test automation

Bonus/Preferred Skills

  • Understanding of wireless networking (802.11, BLE, Zigbee)
  • Experience with AI, machine learning, or software development tools
  • Ability to code in Java, Kotlin, JavaScript, or similar languages
  • Data structures, algorithms knowledge
  • Certifications (CCNA), experience with Cisco/networking products

Why Cisco?

  • Collaborate with a diverse, talented group of engineers
  • Innovate at the networking industry’s forefront
  • Enjoy a culture of teamwork, learning, and global opportunity

If you meet most of the requirements, Cisco encourages you to apply—background diversity is welcomed!

How to Apply

Eligible candidates should apply online using the official link below. Be sure to submit an updated resume and all relevant academic details. Selection is ongoing, so apply as soon as possible.
