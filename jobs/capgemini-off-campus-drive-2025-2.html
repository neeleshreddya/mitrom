About Capgemini

Capgemini is a global leader in consulting, technology services, and digital transformation. With over 350,000 employees in more than 50 countries, Capgemini is committed to innovation and helping clients succeed in the digital age.

Job Overview

Capgemini is conducting its Off Campus Drive 2025 to recruit fresh graduates for the position of Software Engineer. This opportunity is ideal for candidates who want to start their career in software development and work with cutting-edge technologies.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and deploy software solutions following best practices.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and deliver quality results.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous improvement.
  • Maintain and enhance existing applications as needed.
  • Stay updated with emerging technologies and apply them effectively.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduates from 2023, 2024, and 2025 batches.
  • B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, Electronics, or related fields.
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in academics.
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Knowledge of programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.
  • No active backlogs.

Selection Process

  1. Online aptitude and logical reasoning test.
  2. Technical interview rounds focusing on coding and domain knowledge.
  3. HR interview to assess cultural fit and communication skills.
  4. Final offer and onboarding.

Salary & Benefits

Capgemini offers competitive salary packages for freshers starting from ₹13 LPA to ₹18 LPA based on candidate profile. Additional benefits include health insurance, paid leaves, and learning & development opportunities.
