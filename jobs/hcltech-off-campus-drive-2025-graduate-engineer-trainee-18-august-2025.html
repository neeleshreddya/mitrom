HCLTech Off Campus Drive 2025 – Graduate Engineer Trainee

Posted on: July 20, 2025

HCLTech is inviting applications from fresh engineering graduates for the role of Graduate Engineer Trainee. This opportunity is open to candidates from the 2025 batch with specializations in CSE, IT, EEE, ECE, or EIE.

Job Highlights

  • Position: Graduate Engineer Trainee
  • Qualification Required: B.E/B.Tech (CSE, IT, EEE, ECE, EIE)
  • Batch Eligible: 2025
  • Experience: Freshers
  • Expected Salary: ₹4.1 LPA
  • Job Locations: Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Noida
  • Application Deadline: Apply as early as possible

Role Overview

The Graduate Engineer Trainee program at HCLTech is designed to give fresh graduates an opportunity to work on live projects and gain exposure to modern technologies. As part of the role, selected candidates will be trained and deployed across various projects depending on business requirements.

This is not just a job—it is a career pathway that offers long-term growth and skill development.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.E/B.Tech in CSE, IT, EEE, ECE, or EIE
  • 2025 graduate batch only
  • Minimum 65% marks in 10th, 12th, and UG degree
  • Willingness to relocate across India for project needs

Interview Locations: Noida, Pune, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad

Why Choose HCLTech?

  • Work with one of the fastest-growing technology companies
  • Opportunity to contribute to real-world projects with global impact
  • Structured training and learning programs for freshers
  • A supportive environment that encourages growth and innovation

Application Process

Interested and eligible candidates should apply online through the official HCLTech career portal. Since the recruitment process is already active, candidates are advised to submit their applications as soon as possible.
