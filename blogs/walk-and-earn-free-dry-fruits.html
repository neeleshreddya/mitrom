About Koshiqa

Koshiqa is a unique fitness rewards app that turns your daily steps into real rewards — like free dry fruits! The concept is simple: walk 10,000 steps, earn Koshiqa Koins, and redeem them for healthy snacks, medals, and more.

How It Works

  • Download the Koshiqa app from the official website.
  • Connect it to Google Fit for step tracking: Google Fit link
  • Complete 10,000 steps in a day to unlock a reward medal.
  • Earn Koshiqa Koins which you can redeem for free dry fruits and other goodies.
  • Use referral code a143jr when signing up to get bonus rewards instantly.

Why Choose Koshiqa?

In a world where fitness apps are everywhere, Koshiqa stands out by offering tangible rewards. Instead of just counting your steps, it motivates you with something delicious and healthy — dry fruits! Whether you’re health-conscious or just love free stuff, this app makes walking fun and rewarding.

Getting Started

  1. Install the Koshiqa app from the official link.
  2. Sign up using referral code a143jr for bonus Koins.
  3. Walk daily and hit the 10,000-step milestone.
  4. Claim your medals and redeem free dry fruits.

Extra Tips

  • Invite friends to earn extra Koins (use referral code sharing).
  • Combine with morning or evening walks for better health.
  • Keep Google Fit synced for accurate tracking.
